UX Design Summative

Burger Fuel, faced with the UX design challenge of developing a self-ordering system, is a fast-food chain recognized for its dedication to both culinary excellence and customer satisfaction. Burger fuel aims to create a user-friendly and e client self ordering system.

Burger Fuel's unique blend of gourmet burger offerings and innovative menu items will require a UX design solution that not only simplifies order placement but also seamlessly integrates real-time order tracking, ensuring a delightful and convenient interaction.

    App Design Summative

    My team and I were tasked with creating a C2C (Consumer-to-Consumer) web application that streamlines the process of buying and selling products. This online marketplace will serve as a platform for individual users to list their products, connect with potential buyers, and conduct transactions effortlessly. The primary objectives include enhancing user experience, fostering trust, and building a vibrant community centred around the exchange of various products. The website will incorporate CRUD functionalities, enabling users to Create, Read, Update, and Delete items as needed.
